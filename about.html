About us

Who we are

PawsHome connects loving families with homeless pets across Oman. We work with shelters to ensure safe, happy adoptions and promote responsible pet ownership.

Our mission

To create a community where every animal finds a loving home and receives proper care.

What we do

Our team puts forth endless effort to rescue stray animals, rehabilitate them, and get them ready for adoption.Additionally, we use online resources,workshops,and events to teach the community about responsible pet ownership.

Our Team

PawsHome is run by volunteers, veterinarians, foster families, and animal lovers who dedicate their time to improving the lives of pets every day.
